Classic Spanish Omelette!

    Prep time:
  • Less than 5 minutes

    • Cooking time:
  • 20-25 minutes

    • Eating time:
  • Saviour every bite

In order to make the dish for 2 people, we need simple table salt, 4 eggs, 1 whole onion and pepper. You can add in any other herbs or spices you like

Peal your potatoes and then slice thinly, but not to thin! Same goes for your onions. I have a handy slicing maching found on Amazon. ;)

Next, we put atleast 2 TSPs of Spanish (or any other) Olive Oil into a frying pan. Bring the pan to a nice heat where it wont burn the potatoes or onions, we just want them nice a crispy.

Be sure to season well! This time I am personally using salt and pepper. Keepy frying your potatoes and onions until they are almost see through.

Time to crack some eggs! My 4 eggs are cracked into a jar and then I mixed the contents of the frying pan in the jar with the eggs. As you can see my work space is quite messey after this. You can use a bowl to avoid the mess :)

Add another 2TPSs of oil to your pan and bring back to a medium heat. Start gentley pouring the mixture into the frying pan.

Now comes the SUPER messy section. Cook the mixture as you would for any omelette slightly moving the edges to allow the eggs be consistent on all parts. Once the underside is showing some color, we need to flip it to cook the other side. I wouldn't recommend doing this by flipping it in the pan, unless you like cleaning eggs from the floor. Use a large plate or what I've done is use the lid from the frying pan which we will see in the next image.

Lets flip it!

Once both sides are cooked to your liking, you should end with something like this! ENJOY :)
