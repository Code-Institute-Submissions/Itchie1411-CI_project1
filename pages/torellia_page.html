Classic Spanish Omelette!

    Prep time:
  • Less than 5 minutes

    • Cooking time:
  • 20-25 minutes

    • Eating time:
  • Saviour every bite

In order to make the dish for 2 people, we need simple table salt, 4 eggs, 1 whole onion and pepper. You can add in any other herbs or spices you like

Slice your potatoes quite thin. We use a kitchen gadet bought from Amazon to speed this process up
